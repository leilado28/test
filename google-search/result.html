How many types of political consulting are out there?

There are a total of 16 different types of political consulting services that one political party can find.

- Ballot Measure/Ballot Initiative: means by which citizens may propose to create, amend, or repeal a state law or constitutional provision through collecting petition signatures from a certain minimum number of registered voters.

- General Consulting/Campaign Management: planning, tracking analysis and execution of all of the factors involved in a standard politilcal marketing initiative

- Digital: help campaigns create and implement strategies in marketing, public relations, web development, branding and related digital fields.

- Data and analytics: use data sets and models "to draw meaningful insights and solve problems"

- Campaign TechnologyC: any tool or service specifically designed to aid the operation of political campaigns such as donation platforms and volunteer management. Examples: NGP Van, ActBlue, Impactive.

- Database and File Management: maintain a structured analysis of data gathered. The company oversees mainframe security and client-server specifications to optimize productivity.

- Direct Mail and Print: assists with the process of production, printing and sending personalized direct mails to voters and donors.

- Fundraising: provide independent advice and support in developing and/or executing strategic, operational plans related to Fundraising.

- Grassroot Organizing: a method of mobilization that comes from the ground up, everyday people come together to address an issue in their community and take action to advocate for change. They can do this in a variety of ways, including fundraising, creating and sharing petitions, canvassing, and protesting.

- Legal: provides legal guidance to campaigns.

- Media Buying and Placement: the process of purchasing ad space and time on digital and offline platforms, such as websites, YouTube, radio, and TV

- Media Consulting and production: Produce media materials for various digital space.

- Polling/Research: Companies that assist campaigns with conducting research related to polling results.

- Press, Public and Media Relations: work with the marketing or public relations team to conduct press releases, develop media campaigns, and advise executives with their speech during interviews and other related public appearances.

- Public Affairs and Communications: fall under the broad category of public affairs and communications and focus on maintaining a positive public image for their company or organization.

- Telephone Voter Contact: Companies that are responsible for communicating information to voters through telephone.

Source: American Association Of Political Consulant
